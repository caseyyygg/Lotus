Refund Policy

At Lotus Coffee, we are committed to serving you high-quality coffee drinks, frappes, and freshly baked pastries. Due to the nature of our consumable products, we do not accept returns.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or does not meet your expectations, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order at lotusbrewcafe@gmail.com with your order details and clear photos of the issue.

We will assess your request and may offer a refund or replacement depending on the situation.

Food Safety and Hygiene

Because our products are perishable, we cannot accept any items back once they have left our store or delivery service. Please ensure items are stored and consumed promptly and safely upon receipt.

Questions or Concerns

If you have any questions regarding your order or our refund policy, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team.
